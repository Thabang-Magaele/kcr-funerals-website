FAQ

Your Questions
Answered

We've compiled answers to the most common questions our clients ask.

Ask Us Anything
How much does funeral cover cost?

Funeral cover starts from as little as R160 per month. Planning ahead gives you and your family peace of mind in times of uncertainty. Contact us for a plan tailored to your specific needs and budget.

How long before my plan matures?

You can utilise the funeral services after a period of three months. This allows sufficient time for the registration process and ensures your cover is fully in force and ready when you need it.

How quickly are claims processed?

Our simplified claim process is carried out within two working days. The exact timeframe can vary slightly depending on your location, but we aim to resolve every claim as swiftly as possible.

Are there any hidden or additional costs?

No, there are no additional costs. The plan fee covers all the funeral services, tools, and necessary arrangements for your loved one. What you sign up for is what you receive.

Do you offer body repatriation services?

Yes, we offer professional body repatriation services across borders and provinces. Our team handles all logistics with the highest standards of dignity and care.

Where are your offices located?

Our main office is at 105 Orion Building, Bester Street, Nelspruit, 1200. We also have satellite offices in Baberton, Matsulu, Luphisi, Mbuzini, Bushbuckridge and Graskop to serve communities across the region.
