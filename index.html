FAQ

Your Questions Answered

We've compiled answers to the most common questions our clients ask.

How long before my plan matures?

You can utilise the funeral services after a period of three months. This allows sufficient time for the process of registration and ensures your cover is fully in force and ready when you need it.

How quickly are claims processed?

Our simplified claim process is carried out within two working days. The exact timeframe can vary slightly depending on your location, but we aim to resolve every claim as swiftly as possible.

Are there any hidden or additional costs?

No, there are no additional costs. The plan fee covers all the funeral services, tools, and necessary arrangements for your loved one. What you sign up for is what you receive.

Is the funeral fleet safe to use?

Absolutely. Our vehicles are maintained to the highest standards, are non-toxic, and are fully suitable for funeral services. We follow strict safety guidelines and best practices to ensure a safe and respectful burial.

Is food provided during the service?

Yes, food options are available during the course of services when required. Our team can provide full information on catering options and associated details so your family is well cared for throughout.

How do I select the right plan for my family?

Contact us and one of our dedicated consultants will guide you with care and professionalism. We'll assess your family's needs and recommend the most suitable coverage from our range of plans.
